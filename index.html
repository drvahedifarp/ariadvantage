About Dr. Vahedifar

Personalized Care
You Can Trust

Dr. Payman Vahedifar is an internist and nephrologist serving patients in Century City, Beverly Hills, West Los Angeles, Santa Monica, and Culver City. With over 20 years of experience, he provides attentive, individualized care for adults and seniors.

Dr. Vahedifar trained at UCLA and brings subspecialty expertise in kidney disease, resistant hypertension, and diabetes-related conditions. Patients choose this practice for detailed evaluation, same-week availability, and direct access to their physician.

Specialties

Comprehensive
Medical Services

From chronic kidney disease management to annual physicals and surgical clearance, Dr. Vahedifar provides expert care across a broad range of internal medicine and nephrology conditions.

Chronic Kidney Disease
Comprehensive management of CKD, elevated creatinine, proteinuria, and diabetic kidney disease. Slowing progression and preserving kidney function.
Resistant Hypertension
Expert evaluation and management of difficult-to-control high blood pressure, including secondary causes and end-organ protection strategies.
Diabetes Management
Type 2 diabetes care including medication review, labs, kidney health monitoring, and comprehensive chronic disease management.
Internal Medicine
Primary care for adults and seniors including chronic disease management, preventive care, and coordination of complex medical conditions.
Annual Physicals
Comprehensive annual exams and Medicare wellness visits including labs, screenings, and preventive health evaluations.
Pre-Op Clearance
Same-week pre-operative medical clearance including ECG, labs, risk assessment, and surgical coordination for upcoming procedures.
